About Us

This candle company was found by Hong Nguyen from New Orleans, Louisiana.

The creator, Hong, has studied and worked in the healthcare industry in the US for the last 10 years. She is very passionate about creative outlets (contemporary dancing, art, yoga, cooking, photography, etc) and she *tries* to be mindful and sustainable with her lifestyle and business choices as much as possible in her means. She started candle-making due to her interest in the science behind candle-making and as a way to recycle glassware.

Crescent Candles crafts all natural, organic soy candles infused with non-toxic, phthalate-free oils. Self-care products should not be harmful to your health, and this candle line was created mindfully with that intention. Soy is a biodegradable wax that is eco-friendly and nontoxic to your health. Additionally, it lasts longer due to a slower burn and emits less black soot resulting in a cleaner experience.

This line is currently avaliable on the Neutral Ground market every Saturday in New Orleans, Louisiana and during business hours at Galaxy Nails Salon in West Point, Mississippi.

Organic soy waxes that is 100% natural and biodegradable. Hand-poured in small batches for quality.

Therapeutic blends for aromatherapy and practical functions such as odor eliminator for pet smell, smoke eliminator, mosquito repellent, and etc.

Always paraben, phthalate, and paraffin free. Blended with essential oils for maximum health benefits.
